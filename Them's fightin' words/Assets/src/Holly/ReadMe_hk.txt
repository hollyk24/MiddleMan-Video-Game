Overview:
This feature will be the menus used throughout the game. This will include the opening menu and the pause menus both within and outside of battles.

Menus:
	Start Menu 
	Pause outside of battle
	Pause inside of battle


Start Menu:
Will include the play button, instruction button, and setting changes. The setting changes would include difficulty and what player is being used. 

Pause outside of Battle:
This will allow you to pause any dialogue that is taking placed between characters, and will allow you to:
	Resume
	Instructions/help
	Quit
	Difficulty changes

Pause inside of Battle:
This will allow you to pause the battle you are in. Will allow you to resume the battle, and give access to a small help menu on how the battle works.
